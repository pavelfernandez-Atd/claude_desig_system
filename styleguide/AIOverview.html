INSIGHTS

Vyepti's mentions are up +22% while citations have dropped -16% — AI Overviews are referencing Vyepti more frequently in migraine treatment queries, but fewer responses are attributing sources back to vyeptihcp.com. This pattern suggests LLMs are drawing on general medical knowledge rather than the brand's own pages, limiting Vyepti's authority signal relative to competitors like Aimovig and Emgality.

ACTIONS
